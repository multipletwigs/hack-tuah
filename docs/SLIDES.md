## Style Notes

- Use one screenshot per slide where possible.
- Prefer full-width screenshots with minimal cropping.
- Keep headlines under 8 words.
- Keep support text under 14 words.
- Avoid paragraphs.
- Use consistent browser framing.
- Highlight the important UI area with a subtle outline or callout.
